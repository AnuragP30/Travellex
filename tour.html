Holidays By Destinations

Travellex is a premier boutique Destination Management company headquartered in Delhi, founded by seasoned travel enthusiasts and entrepreneurs. With a passion for exploration and adventure, Travellex has become known for providing unforgettable travel experiences that showcase the best of India. Our dedication to personalized service and in-depth knowledge of local destinations ensures our travelers have unique and enriching journeys.

Specializing in adventure tours, Travellex offers an array of activities that cater to all kinds of adventurers. From mountaineering and trekking expeditions in the Indian Himalayas to camel safaris through the Thar Desert, we provide diverse experiences that immerse travelers in India’s natural beauty. Our services also include jeep safaris, bicycle tours, wildlife encounters, and scenic river cruises, ensuring there’s something memorable for everyone.
